Research Interests

What I'm currently working on.

My research focuses on topological quantum matter, with an emphasis on the Kitaev honeycomb spin liquid model. I study how inter-bond Majorana interactions beyond nearest-neighbor hopping affect the bulk topology and edge physics of these systems.

Key topics include mean-field decoupling and self-consistent determination of order parameters, Chern number and Berry curvature diagnostics, and edge-mode analysis via strip geometry and localization measures such as IPR.
